About Knowledge-Centered Service (KCS)

Knowledge-Centered Service (KCS) is a methodology that integrates knowledge creation and maintenance into the customer support process, improving service efficiency and customer satisfaction.

KCS Principles

Capture Knowledge as a By-Product of Work

KCS encourages documenting solutions and knowledge while resolving problems, reducing duplication and enhancing information availability.

Evolve Content Based on Demand and Usage

Knowledge articles are continually improved based on user feedback and real-world application, ensuring accuracy and relevance.

Create a Knowledge-Centered Culture

Organizations adopting KCS foster collaboration and shared responsibility to optimize knowledge sharing and service delivery.
